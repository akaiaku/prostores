Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Overview

QPlay ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our video player application.

Data Collection and Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Video file paths and URLs you add
  • Watch history and playback progress
  • Generated video thumbnails
  • App preferences and settings
  • Subtitle preferences and timing adjustments

Local data is not sent to our servers. However, third-party advertising networks may collect data as described below.

Advertising

This app displays advertisements to support development and maintenance. Third-party advertising networks may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID, IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Ad viewing and click information

These advertising partners have their own privacy policies governing data collection and use. We recommend reviewing their policies:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Other ad network policies as applicable

You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (iOS: Limit Ad Tracking, Android: Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Network Usage

The app may access the network for:

  • Streaming videos from URLs you provide
  • Downloading subtitles from third-party services
  • Loading video metadata and thumbnails

Network requests are made only for features you explicitly use. No analytics or tracking data is sent.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To read and play video files from your device
  • Network Access: To stream online videos and download subtitles
  • Cache Storage: To store thumbnails and temporary files

Third-Party Services

When you use subtitle download features, the app may connect to third-party subtitle databases. These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their terms.

Data Control

You have control over your local data:

  • Clear cache through Settings → Clear Cache
  • Clear watch history through Settings → Clear Watch History
  • Remove videos from your library at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

For advertising data:

  • Reset your Advertising ID in device settings
  • Enable "Limit Ad Tracking" (iOS) or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" (Android)
  • Manage ad preferences through ad network settings

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Parents should monitor their children's use of the application.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app store.

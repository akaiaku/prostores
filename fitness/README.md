Fitness Hub is a simple workout tracker that helps you stay active every day.

🏃 WORKOUT RECORDING
Record your runs, walks, and cycling sessions with live GPS tracking. See your route on a map, track your pace, distance, and calories in real time. A lock screen notification keeps your stats visible while your screen is off.

📊 DAILY DASHBOARD
Check today's steps, distance, active minutes, and calories at a glance. A weekly chart shows your progress across the past 7 days.

🗺️ ROUTE MAP
Every recorded workout saves a GPS route you can review later. Zoom in, pan around, and see exactly where you went.

🔗 HEALTH CONNECT
Optionally sync your steps, heart rate, and calories with Health Connect. You stay in control — grant or revoke access at any time.

🎯 GOALS
Set daily targets for steps, distance, calories, and active minutes. The app notifies you when you reach a goal.

📁 YOUR DATA, YOUR DEVICE
All workout data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. Deleting the app removes everything.

PERMISSIONS USED

• Location — to track your GPS route during a workout
• Physical Activity — to count steps accurately
• Notifications — to show live stats on the lock screen during recording
• Health Connect (optional) — to read and write fitness data

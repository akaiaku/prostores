Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
1. Data We Collect

Fitness Hub may collect the following data solely to power app features:

  • GPS location — to map routes and measure distance during workouts
  • Physical activity — the app uses Activity Recognition to detect walking, running, and cycling in order to count steps and calories accurately
  • Step count — via device motion sensor or Health Connect
  • Heart rate — if available through Health Connect
  • Workout sessions — duration, distance, calories, and notes you enter
  • Body weight & goals — values you manually enter in the app
2. How Data Is Stored

All workout data (sessions, routes, goals) is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database and is never uploaded to any server by Fitness Hub. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data. Note: third-party SDKs embedded in the app (see sections 3 and 7) may independently transmit limited, non-identifiable data to their own servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Health Connect (Android) — syncing step count, heart rate, and calories (only if you enable it)
  • Google AdMob — displays in-app ads. The AdMob SDK may collect the Advertising ID and approximate device location for ad relevance. No precise GPS coordinates are shared.
  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous screen-navigation events (no personal data or location coordinates are logged).

These services are governed by their own privacy policies. Fitness Hub does not control or have access to data collected by those SDKs beyond what is described here.

4. Location Data

GPS location is accessed, collected, and used only during an active workout session (after you explicitly grant permission and press Start). Location is not collected in the background outside of an active workout recording. Route coordinates are stored on-device only and are never transmitted by Fitness Hub. The Google AdMob SDK (used for in-app advertising) may independently use the device's approximate location for ad targeting — this is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, not Fitness Hub. You can limit this in your device's Privacy → Ads settings.

5. Notifications

If you enable Activity Notifications, the app schedules local push notifications for daily step reminders and activity summaries. These are generated entirely on-device — no personal data is sent to a notification server.

6. Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time via Settings → Data & Storage → Clear All Data, or by uninstalling the app. You may revoke Health Connect permissions at any time through the Health Connect app.

7. Advertising

This app displays ads via Google AdMob. The AdMob SDK may collect and use:

  • Advertising ID — a resettable, anonymous device identifier used for ad personalization.
  • Approximate device location — used by Google to serve geographically relevant ads. Fitness Hub does not share your precise GPS workout coordinates with AdMob.
  • App usage signals — such as which screens you visit, used to improve ad relevance.

No personally identifiable information (name, email, exact GPS coordinates) is shared with advertisers by Fitness Hub. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please reach out via the app's support page or the developer contact listed on the Play Store.

By using Fitness Hub, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy from time to time — the latest version is always available in the app.

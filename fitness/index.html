Fitness Hub

A simple workout tracker that helps you stay active every day — runs, walks, cycling, steps & more.

Google Play Privacy Policy

Features

🏃 Workout Recording

Record runs, walks, and cycling with live GPS. See your route, pace, distance, and calories in real time.

📊 Daily Dashboard

Check today's steps, distance, active minutes, and calories at a glance with a 7-day weekly chart.

🗺️ Route Map

Every workout saves a GPS route you can review later. Zoom in, pan around, and see exactly where you went.

🔗 Health Connect

Optionally sync steps, heart rate, and calories with Health Connect. Grant or revoke access at any time.

🎯 Goals

Set daily targets for steps, distance, calories, and active minutes. Get notified when you hit a goal.

📁 Your Data, Your Device

All workout data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. Deleting the app removes everything.

Permissions Used

📍 Location To track your GPS route during a workout session
🚶 Physical Activity To count steps accurately via Activity Recognition
🔔 Notifications To show live stats on the lock screen during recording
❤️ Health Connect Optional — to read and write fitness data (steps, heart rate, calories)

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how QuickMind collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Notes and Content

  • Notes Content: All notes, text, images, and attachments you create
  • Folders: Your folder structure and note organization
  • Templates: Custom templates you create
  • Formatting: Rich text formatting and styles

1.2 App Settings

  • Preferences: Theme preferences (dark/light mode)
  • Font Settings: Selected fonts and sizes
  • Language: Your preferred language

1.3 Device Information

  • Device Type: Device model and OS version
  • App Usage: Features you use to improve functionality
  • Error Logs: Crash reports for app stability

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Note Storage: Saving and managing your notes locally on your device
  • Organization: Organizing notes in folders and with tags
  • Customization: Applying your theme, font, and language preferences
  • App Improvement: Improving app stability and performance through error logs

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All your notes and data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your notes, content, or personal information to external servers.

3.1 Security Measures

  • AES-256 Encryption: Password-protected notes use military-grade encryption
  • Secure Password Hashing: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Local SQLite Database: Notes stored in secure local database
  • App-Specific Storage: Data isolated in app-specific directory

3.2 Password Protection

  • Individual notes can be protected with passwords
  • Encrypted notes use AES-256 encryption
  • You have full control over which notes are protected
  • We cannot recover forgotten passwords (for your security)

4. Permissions Explained

4.1 Required Permissions

  • Storage: To save notes, images, and app data locally
  • Camera: To capture photos for notes (optional feature)

4.2 Optional Permissions

  • Gallery/Photos: To insert images from your photo library
  • Notifications: To remind you about notes (if you enable reminders)

5. No Advertising or Tracking

No Ads, No Tracking: QuickMind does not display advertisements and does not track your behavior. We do not use analytics services or collect personal identifiable information.

5.1 What We Don't Collect

  • No Advertising ID: We don't collect or use advertising identifiers
  • No User Tracking: We don't track your behavior or app usage patterns
  • No Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics or similar services
  • No Third-party SDKs: No advertising or tracking SDKs integrated

6. Data Sharing

We Never Share Your Data: Your notes, content, and personal information are never shared with anyone. All data remains private and is stored only on your device.

6.1 No Third-Party Services

  • No Cloud Services: We don't use cloud storage or sync services
  • No Analytics Partners: No data shared with analytics providers
  • No Advertising Networks: No data shared with ad networks
  • Complete Privacy: Your notes stay on your device only

7. Your Rights and Controls

7.1 Data Control

  • Access: View all your notes through the app interface
  • Export: Share notes as text, PDF, or other formats
  • Delete: Delete individual notes or clear all app data
  • Modify: Edit notes, folders, and settings at any time

7.2 Permission Management

  • Revoke app permissions through device settings
  • App functions with minimal required permissions
  • Optional features require optional permissions only

7.3 Data Deletion

Complete Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all data from your device. You can also clear data through device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

QuickMind is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, as all data is stored locally on the device.

8.1 Family-Friendly App

  • No ads or in-app purchases
  • No online features or social components
  • All data stored locally on device
  • Suitable for students and learners of all ages

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Notification of Changes: We will notify users of significant changes through the app or app store updates.

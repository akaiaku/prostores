Pulse Remote

Control your Smart TV from your phone — Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, voice commands & more, all in one app.

Google Play Privacy Policy

Features

📺 Smart TV Control

Control your Android TV, Sony, Samsung and more over Wi-Fi — navigate menus, adjust volume, and change channels instantly.

🔵 Bluetooth Support

Seamlessly switch to Bluetooth when Wi-Fi isn't available. Stay in control even without a shared network.

🎤 Voice Commands

Use your voice to search, launch apps, and control your Android TV or Sony TV via Google Assistant — hands-free.

🕹️ D-Pad & Touchpad

Switch between classic D-pad navigation and a smooth touchpad mode. Pick whichever feels natural to you.

📱 App Launcher

Create custom shortcuts to your favourite TV apps. Launch Netflix, YouTube, Spotify and more with a single tap.

🔧 Multi-Device Manager

Save and switch between multiple TVs and devices. Your pairings are stored locally — no account needed.

Permissions Used

📡 Local Network To discover and connect to your TV over Wi-Fi
🔵 Bluetooth To connect to devices via Bluetooth when Wi-Fi is unavailable
🎤 Microphone For voice commands — audio is streamed directly to your TV, never stored
🔔 Notifications Optional — to surface connection status and alerts

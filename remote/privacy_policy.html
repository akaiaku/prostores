Privacy Policy

Pulse Remote  ·  Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Pulse Remote ("the App") is a TV remote control application that lets you control smart TVs and other devices over your local Wi-Fi network. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly what data the App collects, why, and how it is handled.

Short version  The App does not collect any personally identifiable information. Anonymous data may be collected by Google AdMob (ads), Firebase Analytics (usage), and Firebase Crashlytics (crash reports) as described below.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account registration or login is required.
  • No personal information (name, email, location) is collected by us.
  • Voice input processed via Google Assistant stays entirely on-device and on the TV — we never receive or store it.
  • Device discovery data (IP addresses, device names) is used in-memory only and never leaves your device.

Local Network Access

The App communicates directly with your TV over your local Wi-Fi network. All communication is peer-to-peer on your private network. No traffic is routed through our servers.

Microphone Permission

The microphone is used exclusively to enable voice commands to your TV (Google Assistant on Android TV / Sony). Audio is streamed directly from your device to your TV. We do not record, store, or transmit your voice to any server.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain anonymous identifiers and usage data to serve contextual ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device's ad settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads on Android).

Analytics & Crash Reporting (Firebase)

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, provided by Google. These services collect anonymous usage data and crash reports to help us improve app stability and user experience.

  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous event data such as screen views and feature usage. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash logs and device state at the time of a crash. Reports do not contain personal data.

All data collected by Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of analytics data collection by disabling it in your device's app settings or by resetting your advertising ID.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App saves the following preferences locally using SharedPreferences:

  • Paired TV / device list (IP addresses and names of devices you have added).
  • UI preferences (basic mode, D-pad / touchpad mode choice).
  • Custom app shortcuts you create in the app launcher.

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party SDKs:

  • Google AdMob — advertising.
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK — ad rendering.
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting and stability monitoring.

No social-media SDKs are included.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

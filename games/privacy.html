Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1Our Commitment

ProStores Games respects your privacy. This policy clearly explains what information we collect (and what we do not collect), and how that information is used.

🛡️ ProStores Games does not require account registration, does not collect personal information, and does not sell user data to third parties.

2What We Collect

The table below describes exactly what is stored when you play our games:

Data type Description Where stored Status
High score Your best score in each game Browser localStorage ✓ Device only
Game settings Volume, display preferences Browser localStorage ✓ Device only
IP address Basic access logs (via hosting provider) Server logs Automatic · Anonymous
Personal information Name, email, address, etc. — ✗ Not collected
Tracking cookies Analytics or behavioral cookies — ✗ Not used

3LocalStorage & Cookies

Our games use the browser's localStorage API to save high scores and game settings. This data:

  • Lives only on your device — it is never sent to our servers
  • Is not shared with any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser's site data
  • Contains no personally identifiable information

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any other tracking technology.

4Third-Party Resources

This site uses Google Fonts (Inter) for typography. When fonts are loaded, your browser connects to Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's own privacy policy.

Beyond Google Fonts, ProStores Games loads no third-party resources — no ads, no external JavaScript CDNs, no tracking pixels.

5Children's Privacy

ProStores Games does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

6Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for commercial purposes. Data is handled internally and only to the extent necessary to operate the service.

The only exception is a legally binding request from a competent governmental authority.

7Your Rights

You have full control over the data stored on your device:

  • View data: Open DevTools → Application → Local Storage to inspect saved game data
  • Delete data: Clear localStorage or remove all site data in your browser settings
  • Prevent storage: Use Private / Incognito mode — data is discarded automatically when you close the tab

8Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Questions about privacy? We're here to help.

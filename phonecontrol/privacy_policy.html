Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Call Filter Pro collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Contact Information

  • Contacts Data: We access your device's contact list to provide call blocking and contact management features
  • Call Logs: We read call history to identify blocked numbers and provide call statistics
  • Phone Numbers: We process phone numbers for blocking and identification purposes

1.2 Device Information

  • Device Permissions: We request permissions necessary for app functionality (contacts, phone, notifications)
  • App Usage: We may collect information about how you use the app to improve functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Call Blocking: Identifying and blocking unwanted calls based on your preferences
  • Contact Management: Organizing and managing your contact list
  • App Functionality: Providing core features like call logs, backup, and sync
  • Notifications: Sending relevant notifications about blocked calls and app updates

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your contacts, call logs, or personal information to external servers.

3.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in secure, app-specific storage areas
  • Access Control: Only the app can access its stored data

3.2 Backup and Sync

  • Backup files are encrypted and stored locally or in your chosen cloud storage
  • You have full control over backup creation and restoration
  • No data is transmitted without your explicit consent

4. Permissions Explained

4.1 Required Permissions

  • Contacts: To read and manage your contact list for blocking and organization
  • Phone: To access call logs and implement call blocking functionality
  • Storage: To save app data, settings, and backup files
  • Notifications: To notify you about blocked calls and app status

4.2 Optional Permissions

  • Microphone: Only if you enable voice-related features (currently not implemented)
  • Camera: For scanning QR codes or taking photos for contacts (if implemented)

5. Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use the following data:

5.1 Advertising Data Collection

  • Advertising ID: Used to serve personalized ads and prevent fraud
  • Device Information: Screen size, device model, OS version for ad optimization
  • Usage Data: App interactions for ad targeting and performance measurement
  • Location: Approximate location for location-based ads (optional, with your consent)
  • IP Address: For fraud prevention and general location targeting

5.2 Ad Network Partners

  • Google AdMob: Primary advertising platform
  • Google Analytics: For app usage analytics and ad performance
  • Third-party advertisers: May receive aggregated, non-personal data

5.3 Your Ad Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • You can reset your advertising ID anytime through device settings
  • Location-based ads can be disabled in app settings
  • You can limit ad tracking in your device privacy settings
Ad Controls: Go to Settings > Privacy > Ads on your device to manage ad preferences, or use the ad controls within our app settings.

For more information about Google's advertising practices, visit: Google Privacy Policy and Google Play Developer Policy

6. Data Sharing

6.1 Personal Data Protection

Your personal data (contacts, call logs) is never shared. This information remains private and is stored only on your device.

6.2 Advertising Data Sharing

  • Google AdMob: Receives advertising ID, device info, and usage patterns
  • Analytics Partners: Receive aggregated, anonymized usage statistics
  • No Personal Info: Your contacts, phone numbers, and call logs are never shared

6.3 Data Retention by Partners

  • AdMob data: Retained according to Google's data retention policies
  • Analytics data: Retained for service improvement and measurement
  • User control: You can reset advertising ID to limit tracking

7. Your Rights and Controls

7.1 Personal Data Control

  • Access: You can view all data stored by the app through the app's interface
  • Export: You can export your data using the backup feature
  • Delete: You can delete individual entries or clear all app data
  • Modify: You can edit contacts, call blocks, and settings at any time

7.2 Advertising Controls

  • Personalized Ads: Disable in device Settings > Privacy > Ads
  • Reset Advertising ID: Reset in device Settings > Privacy > Ads
  • Location-based Ads: Control in app Settings > Privacy > Location
  • Ad Frequency: Managed automatically by ad networks

7.3 Permission Management

  • You can revoke app permissions at any time through device settings
  • The app will guide you to enable necessary permissions for core functionality
  • You can use the app with limited functionality if some permissions are denied
  • Advertising permissions can be managed separately from core app permissions

7.4 Data Deletion Rights

Right to Delete: You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app or using the "Clear All Data" option in app settings.
  • Local Data: Deleted immediately when you clear app data
  • Advertising Data: Reset advertising ID to limit tracking history
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized data may be retained for service improvement

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8.1 Age Verification

  • App requires mature understanding of call management and privacy settings
  • Parental supervision recommended for users under 18
  • Parents can control ad personalization through device family settings

8.2 Parental Controls

  • Device Controls: Parents can disable ads entirely through device settings
  • App Permissions: Parents can manage app permissions for family devices
  • Data Protection: Enhanced privacy protections apply to family accounts
For Parents: If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us to have it removed immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Notification of Changes: We will notify users of significant changes through the app or app store updates.

9. Open Source and Transparency

Call Filter Pro is committed to transparency:

  • Our app's source code practices data handling responsibly
  • You can verify our privacy claims through code inspection
  • We follow industry best practices for mobile app privacy
